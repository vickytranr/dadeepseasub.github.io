Welcome aboard this anonymous deep sea research vessel.
A couple housekeeping rules, and you can be on your way.

It might take you a while to find your sea legs, but maps
in the STAIRWELLS and NAVIGATION ROOM will help
you find your way. Feel free to explore the ship and
see the kinds of things we get up to around here.

I'd recommend the COMMUNICATION, where you can
broadcast whatever you please into the open sea, and the
OBSERVATION DECK, where you can look through our periscope
and see what anyone else has broadcasted.
